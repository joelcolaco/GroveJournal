# Grove Journal Website

Static public website for Grove Journal.

Production URL: `https://www.joelcolaco.com/GroveJournal`

## Official links

Use these stable English URLs for App Store Connect, Google Play Console, RevenueCat, and in-app legal links:

- Privacy Policy: `https://www.joelcolaco.com/GroveJournal/privacy/`
- Terms of Service: `https://www.joelcolaco.com/GroveJournal/terms/`

The English legal documents are the authoritative versions. Spanish, French, and German translations are provided for convenience.

## What is included

- Plain static HTML/CSS, no build step
- Light Grove Journal visual style
- English, Spanish, French, and German home pages
- Official English Privacy Policy and Terms of Service URLs
- Convenience legal translations for Spanish, French, and German
- GitHub Issue Forms for feature requests and fallback bug reports
- `404.html`, `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, and `.nojekyll`
